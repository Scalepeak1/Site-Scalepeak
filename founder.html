Mahrez Aouadj

Founder & Managing Director, SCALEPEAK LLC

Building consumer brands in the pet care and health & wellness space, with a focus on long-term growth across Europe and the United States.

From idea to infrastructure.

Mahrez started building Kriath with a simple observation: dog owners across Europe were underserved when it came to high-quality, liquid-format supplements. Most products on the market were powders or chews that dogs often refused, or formulations that simply did not absorb well. Kriath was built to solve that specific problem.

What started as a single product line grew into a real business with thousands of customers across France, Belgium and Switzerland. Rather than chase rapid expansion, Mahrez focused on getting the fundamentals right: product quality, customer service, and operational consistency.

SCALEPEAK LLC was incorporated in Wyoming to formalize that work and build the infrastructure needed for the next phase: expanding into new product categories and, eventually, into the United States market.

A few things I believe in.

Build for the customer, not the metrics.

Every decision starts with whether it genuinely improves the customer's experience, not whether it looks good on a dashboard.

Operational discipline compounds.

Small, consistent improvements in how a business runs matter more than any single big move.

Patience is a strategy.

The brands worth building take years, not months. I would rather grow slowly and correctly than quickly and carelessly.

Structure enables ambition.

Getting the legal and financial foundation right, like incorporating SCALEPEAK LLC, is what makes future growth possible.
